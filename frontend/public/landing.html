Questions?

Frequently asked

Do I need to install anything?
No. PanelOS runs in your browser on any laptop, desktop or phone. Just log in — nothing to install or maintain.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data lives securely in the cloud with automated backups and role-based access — far safer than a file on one person's laptop. Each company's data is fully isolated.
Is it GST compliant?
Absolutely. Invoices auto-calculate CGST/SGST/IGST, support HSN codes, amount-in-words, and are e-Invoice (IRN) and e-Way bill ready. You can also export to Tally.
Can my whole team use it together?
Yes — sales, production, stores and accounts all work on one live system with role-based permissions, so everyone sees the right thing and nothing conflicts.
I'm not very technical. Is it hard to use?
PanelOS is built for factory teams, not IT departments. It follows the natural flow of your business, and there's a built-in step-by-step guide with screenshots inside the app.
What happens after the free trial?
You get 14 days free with no credit card. After that, pick a plan that fits — and your data stays exactly where it is. No lock-in.
